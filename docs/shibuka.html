SHIBUKA

Project Shibuka is a community-driven program that cultivates future leaders through personalized leadership training, career exposure, mentorship, and civic engagement. It features customized workshops, mentor matching, and hands-on civic projects. The program is implemented by schools, nonprofits, and youth councils, targeting underserved youth. It reaches participants via school partnerships and digital platforms. Its key strength is a mentorship-driven, tailored approach that fosters personal growth and civic responsibility, empowering youth to make a lasting impact in their communities.
